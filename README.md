# Thinking eternally

Welcome to a transformative journey that transcends the boundaries of time! In a world that often demands our immediate attention and pulls us into the frantic pace of the "now," it is easy to lose sight of the grand, divine design. This course is designed to help you lift your gaze and recalibrate your heart. By rooting our perspective in the restored gospel of Jesus Christ, we will explore how to see the complexities, joys, and even the heartaches of mortality through the lens of eternity.

We aren't just here to survive the day; we are here to thrive and prepare for a glorious future as heirs of God. Throughout this experience, you will gain practical skills to transform your daily walk into a purposeful preparation for the life to come. You will discover that when we "think eternally," the heavy burdens of today become lighter, and the small joys of the present become more vibrant. Whether you are currently navigating the heights of success or the depths of trial, this course will empower you to find the divine purpose woven into every single moment.

## What You Will Achieve

By the conclusion of this course, you will be able to:

*   **Internalize Your Divine Identity:** Deepen your understanding of your heritage as a child of heavenly parents and how that identity dictates your potential.
*   **Apply the Atonement Daily:** Learn to leverage the power of Jesus Christ’s Infinite Atonement to bridge the gap between your mortal imperfections and eternal aspirations.
*   **Reframing Adversity:** Develop the spiritual resilience to see trials not as roadblocks, but as essential training and catalysts for growth.
*   **Cultivate Eternal Habits:** Establish daily rituals and discipleship patterns that keep your mind "stayed on things above."
*   **Master the Skill of Joy:** Practice the spiritual discipline of rejoicing in all circumstances, recognizing the hand of the Lord in every detail of your life.

## Course Journey

*   **Foundations of Eternal Identity:** Understanding our divine origins and the framework of the Plan of Salvation to establish an eternal baseline.
*   **The Infinite Atonement:** Focusing on the centrality of Jesus Christ in bridging the gap between mortality and eternity.
*   **Purposeful Mortality:** Learning to see the specific challenges and opportunities of the physical world as essential training.
*   **Transforming Trials into Triumphs:** Developing the spiritual resilience to find joy and growth in the midst of adversity.
*   **The Eternal Family and Community:** Focusing on the relationships that endure beyond the grave and how to nurture them.
*   **Daily Discipleship and Rituals:** Establishing the habits that keep our minds stayed on things above.
*   **Rejoicing in Every Moment:** Cultivating the spiritual skill of finding joy regardless of external circumstances.
*   **Eternal Legacy and Synthesis:** Preparing for the transition to the next life and completing the course capstone.

## Begin Your Eternal Transformation

Are you ready to change the way you see the world? The shift from a temporal mindset to an eternal perspective is the most significant change a soul can make. It is the key to finding peace that passes all understanding and a joy that remains constant regardless of the storms of life. Step forward with faith, open your heart to the promptings of the Spirit, and let us begin the work of thinking eternally together. Your divine destiny is waiting—let’s start the journey today!
